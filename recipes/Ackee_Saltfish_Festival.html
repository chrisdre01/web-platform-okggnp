Ackee and Saltfish

Description

Ackee & Saltfish with Festival: features a wide variety of spices and seasonings - depending on the cook. The dough is a mix of corn meal and all-purpose flour deep- fried in oil. Festival is one of my favorite comfort foods.


Ingredients

  • 1 can ackee
  • about 2 cups 1/3 lb saltfish
  • boneless/skinless (salted cod or other)
  • 1 medium onion;
  • 1 sliced scotch bonnet pepper
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 1 medium tomato; cubed
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 green onions
  • 1/4 medium red bell pepper
  • cloves garlic SERVE WITH 1 green banana
  • 1 fried ripe plantain
  • 1 fried batch of festival ; or fried dumplings

Steps

  1. Put the dry salted fish to boil in a pot on high heat, then simmer for about 20 minutes.
  2. Drain, rinse under cool water and let the water drain.
  3. Break apart into large flakes.
  4. In a large saucepan, heat the oil.
  5. Add garlic, sliced onions, scotch bonnet pepper.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes until onion softens up.
  7. Add sliced bell pepper, green onion, black pepper, and thyme.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes.
  9. Add flaked saltfish and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  10. Add tomato and stir.
  11. Allow the tomato to warm through for 1-2 minutes.
  12. Drain the ackee and rinse gently with cold water.
  13. Add to the saucepan and use 2 forks to toss with the other ingredients.
  14. Let sit for a few minutes to allow flavours to marry.
